Systems handling millions of transactions Concurrency · reliability under load

Built and hardened the Packages stack for millions of small-business transactions: idempotency, retries, deduplication, and explicit invariants for simultaneous redemptions, refunds, and partial failures on unreliable networks.

Service mesh & cross-service behavior. Production issues often surfaced at service boundaries—timeouts, retry storms, inconsistent state across hops, and hard-to-reproduce races when multiple services touched the same ledger paths. Work included tracing those failure modes, tightening contracts between callers and dependencies, and validating behavior under real merchant traffic—not synthetic benchmarks.
