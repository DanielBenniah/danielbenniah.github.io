About

I specialize in the intersection of distributed systems and applied AI: experimentation at scale, bandit-driven and adaptive experiences, LLM evaluation, and multi-agent reinforcement learning.

My work has driven critical infrastructure and product outcomes at Netflix, Square, and Amazon — from centralized experimentation and ramp frameworks to GenAI-assisted tooling and retention systems. I’m an IEEE Senior Member (a distinction held by roughly 10% of IEEE’s membership), and I’ve been invited to judge industry awards and conference programs, peer-review for leading venues, and contribute to open-source platforms used by thousands of developers worldwide.
