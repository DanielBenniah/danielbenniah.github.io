Contributions

Signal-free coordination for autonomous traffic (6G vehicle-to-vehicle)

Problem. Fixed-cycle signals scale poorly as autonomous density increases; centralized intersection control is brittle. How should vehicles coordinate passage using radio rather than infrastructure-only timing?

Novel contribution. Reinforcement-learning-oriented V2V framing for signal-free intersections, linking 6G-style connectivity to control policies for throughput and emissions, with complementary analysis in the journal literature.

Field relevance. Intelligent transportation and cellular-assisted autonomy; inspectable claims via pending patent filing and peer-reviewed IJACSA article.

Peer-reviewed article (IJACSA)

Decentralized multi-agent RL for scheduling in heterogeneous distributed systems

Problem. Static schedulers and full centralization fail under shifting workloads and partial observability in clusters and edge deployments.

Novel contribution. Dec-POMDP formulation with a lightweight actor–critic implementation (minimal framework dependencies), evaluated against trace-derived workloads.

Field relevance. Resource allocation under heterogeneity and competing objectives; connects to public cluster-trace methodology.

Technical report (arXiv)

Semantic-aware resource control for 6G network slicing

Problem. Slicing requires differentiated QoS; static allocation wastes spectrum when demand and semantics vary.

Novel contribution. GAN-enhanced DDPG formulation for semantic-aware allocation in 6G slicing.

Field relevance. Operator-scale 5G/6G management and edge QoS automation.

Metaheuristic optimization for power-system protection coordination

Problem. Mis-coordinated overcurrent relays worsen fault propagation; classical tuning scales poorly.

Novel contribution. Firefly and improved harmony search applied to relay coordination (CSNT 2016; TechRxiv manuscript).

Field relevance. Grid reliability and protection engineering practice.
